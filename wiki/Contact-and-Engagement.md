# Contact & Engagement

We're excited to learn about your challenges and explore how we can help. Here's how to connect with Szerj Systems.

## Get In Touch

### Start a Conversation

The best way to begin is with a conversation. We offer free initial consultations to:
- Understand your challenges and goals
- Explore potential solutions
- Discuss how we might work together
- Answer your questions about our services

**No pressure, no obligation** - just a chance to see if there's a good fit.

### Contact Methods

**GitHub**
- Open an issue in any of our repositories
- Start a discussion in our community
- Engage with our open-source projects

**Email**
- Reach out directly via email
- Include a brief description of your needs
- We typically respond within 24 hours

**Professional Networks**
- Connect on LinkedIn
- Follow our updates and insights
- Message us directly through professional platforms

## What to Expect

### Initial Consultation

**Duration:** 30-60 minutes

**Format:** Video call or phone call (your preference)

**What We'll Discuss:**
- Your current situation and challenges
- Goals and desired outcomes
- Timeline and constraints
- Potential approaches and solutions
- Next steps (if there's mutual interest)

**What to Prepare:**
- Brief overview of your organization
- Description of challenges you're facing
- Any relevant context about current systems
- Thoughts on timeline and budget (rough is fine)

### After the Consultation

If we determine there's a good fit, we'll:

1. **Proposal** (1-2 weeks)
   - Detailed approach and methodology
   - Scope of work and deliverables
   - Timeline and milestones
   - Cost estimate and payment terms

2. **Follow-up Discussion** (as needed)
   - Answer questions about proposal
   - Refine scope or approach
   - Discuss any concerns

3. **Agreement** (when ready)
   - Finalize statement of work
   - Execute contract
   - Schedule project kickoff

4. **Kickoff** (1-2 weeks after agreement)
   - Begin discovery process
   - Meet the full team
   - Set up communication channels
   - Start the work!

## Types of Engagements

### Project Consultation

**Best For:**
- Exploring a specific initiative
- Need expert input on an idea
- Evaluating technical approaches
- Planning a project

**What's Included:**
- Discovery session(s)
- Technical assessment
- Recommendation report
- Implementation roadmap (high-level)

**Timeline:** 1-2 weeks

### Full Project Implementation

**Best For:**
- Ready to build a solution
- Clear need and general direction
- Committed to making change
- Have budget and timeline in mind

**What's Included:**
- Complete project lifecycle
- Discovery through deployment
- Training and documentation
- Post-launch support period

**Timeline:** 4-16 weeks (typical range)

### Ongoing Partnership

**Best For:**
- Continuous improvement mindset
- Multiple initiatives over time
- Need strategic technology partner
- Want dedicated capacity

**What's Included:**
- Monthly retainer agreement
- Flexible scope and priorities
- Regular strategic sessions
- Mix of strategic and tactical work
- Priority access to our team

**Timeline:** 6-12 month initial commitment

### Advisory Services

**Best For:**
- Have internal technical team
- Need expert perspective
- Facing specific decisions
- Want architecture review

**What's Included:**
- As-needed consultation
- Architecture reviews
- Technology decisions support
- Best practices guidance

**Timeline:** Flexible, as needed

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much do your services cost?

Project costs vary based on scope, complexity, and timeline. We offer:
- **Small projects:** Starting around $10-25K
- **Medium projects:** $25-75K range
- **Large initiatives:** $75K+
- **Monthly retainers:** Starting at $5K/month

We'll provide specific estimates based on your needs.

### Do you work with companies outside your typical industries?

Absolutely! While we highlight certain industries, we work across many sectors. Our approach adapts to different contexts, and we bring valuable cross-industry perspective.

### Can you work with our existing team/vendors?

Yes! We collaborate well with internal teams and other vendors. We can:
- Work alongside your existing team
- Partner with other consultants
- Integrate with current vendors
- Support your staff with training

### Do you offer support after project completion?

Yes. All projects include a post-launch support period. Beyond that, we offer:
- Ongoing support retainers
- Pay-as-you-go support
- Enhancement projects
- Long-term partnerships

### What if we're not sure exactly what we need?

That's common and perfectly fine! The discovery process helps clarify:
- What problems to prioritize
- Which solutions are most appropriate
- What scope and approach make sense
- What timeline and budget are realistic

We can start with a focused consultation to help you figure it out.

### How do you handle intellectual property?

- You own all custom work we create for you
- We retain rights to our proprietary tools and methods
- Open source usage is clearly documented
- All IP terms are explicit in contracts

### Do you sign NDAs?

Yes, we're happy to sign reasonable non-disclosure agreements. We treat all client information as confidential by default.

### What if our project changes direction?

We expect some evolution and build flexibility into our approach:
- Regular checkpoints to reassess
- Agile methodology enables pivots
- Change request process for major shifts
- Open communication about adjustments

### How do we know if we're a good fit?

Good fits typically involve:
- Commitment to improving operations
- Openness to collaboration
- Realistic timeline expectations
- Budget appropriate for scope
- Willingness to invest in change

The initial consultation helps both sides assess fit.

### Can you help with urgent/emergency needs?

We can sometimes accommodate urgent needs, depending on:
- Current team capacity
- Nature and scope of urgency
- Timeline flexibility
- Resources available

Contact us to discuss - we'll be honest about what's possible.
