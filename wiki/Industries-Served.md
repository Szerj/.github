- Data scattered across multiple platforms

**Technology Gaps**

- Uncertainty about technology investments
- Outgrowing basic tools but not ready for enterprise software
- Need for custom solutions on SMB budgets

**Growth Constraints**
- Processes that don't scale
- Inability to handle increased volume
- Quality concerns as operations expand
- Resource constraints limiting expansion

**Integration Solutions**
